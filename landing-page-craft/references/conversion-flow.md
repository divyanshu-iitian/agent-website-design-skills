# Conversion Flow

## Above The Fold

Answer: What is it? Who is it for? Why should I care now? What can I do next?

## Middle

Show mechanism and proof. The user should see how the offer works before being asked to trust broad claims.

## Late Page

Resolve friction: price, risk, setup time, integrations, support, guarantees, security, or eligibility.

## CTA Rules

Use one primary action per decision point. Secondary actions should reduce uncertainty, such as "View demo", "See pricing", or "Read case study".
